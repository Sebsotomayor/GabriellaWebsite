Advocacy

Advocacy through Public Communications


Gabriella Cortes-Alban is a communicator and advocate dedicated to using her voice to inspire dialogue around equity, and community empowerment. Drawing on her interdisciplinary research, she explores today's most pressing issues from multiple angles, seeing how systems can work together rather than apart. Grounded in both experience and academia, Gabriella shares this perspective through peer education, storytelling, and public speaking. She actively engages in community discussions and is launching a podcast that explores how the arts, education, and social innovation intersect to create meaningful change.
