About Me

Gabriella Cortes-Alban

Multisector Strategist: Creating Social Impact through Arts, Public Affairs, and Community Engagement

I'm Gabriella Cortes-Alban, an interdisciplinary professional and performing artist about to graduate with a Bachelor of Science in Interdisciplinary Studies from the University of Central Florida. I blend my studies in commerce, public affairs, nonprofit management, and performing arts administration with my work on stage to unite creativity and strategy in fundraising, program development, and community engagement.

Growing up immersed in culture and community advocacy through my father's immigrant-focused medical practice and my mother's Latin entertainment company, I learned early on that art and service are not opposites but complementary colors on the same canvas.

Working across healthcare, education, and philanthropy, I discovered my purpose where these paths meet, using the arts to bring healing and connection to people from all walks of life.

Today, I'm based in Orlando, performing as a vocalist and dancer on corporate, theme park, and community stages, also leading workshops that help others channel their emotions through art and empowerment.

These experiences shape the creative lens I bring when collaborating with local businesses and nonprofits.

My vision is to challenge society's black-and-white standards and invite people to see life in full color, creating change through the power of art.
