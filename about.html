About Me

Gabriella Cortes-Alban


Multisector Strategist: Creating Social Impact through Arts, Public Affairs, and Community Engagement

Gabriella Cortes-Alban is a senior at the University of Central Florida, pursuing a Bachelor's Degree in Interdisciplinary Studies- integrating commerce, public affairs, nonprofit management, and performing arts administration with the objective of addressing/ tackling/ solving social issues through the power of the arts.

Her professional career in entertainment allows for innovative approaches in fundraising, program development, and public communications to address community needs with passion and purpose.
