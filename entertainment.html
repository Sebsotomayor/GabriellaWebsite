Entertainment

My Work as an Artist

    I blend voice and Latin dance to create medleys that fuse Latin pop, salsa, and Colombian folk, offering performances that are visually captivating and musically immersive. As a lead vocalist and dancer and part of the coordination team for UCF's first Latin Jazz and Tropical Orchestra (debuting November 21, 2025), I bring these shows to community events and beyond.

    Passionate about preserving these genres, I aim to keep the rich musical and dance traditions of our culture alive for my generation, sharing them in ways that are both authentic and fresh.

Collaborations with Entertainment Companies

    I've had the privilege of working with some of Central Florida's leading entertainment companies, including Eklectic Entertainment, StepFlix Entertainment, and Adanse Entertainment—through which I've experienced some of the most rewarding moments of my career.

  • Opening for Pitbull with Eklectic Entertainment
  • Walt Disney World's Fiesta en La Calle with Eklectic Entertainment
  • Ritmo Latino Concert (dancer for Eklectic Entertainment) — performed for over 10,000 attendees at the Orange County Convention Center
